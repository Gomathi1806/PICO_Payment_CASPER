# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community
- No harassment, trolling, personal attacks, or publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the repository owner via GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
